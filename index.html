Army of Two

This Co-op based shooter simulates missions throughout the world,in which there are to characters, and they initiate combat as they are given objectives. The rage that these two characters carry makes them impossible to take down.

Army of Two Sample Gameplay

Tekken 5

This 1v1 style fighting Game, staring Japanese fictional characters is much like Mortal Combat, Streetfighter, and Super Smash Bros Melee. You can play in a story mode, which allows you to fight amongst the ranks, or you can engage in combat against a friend in the versus game mode.

Tekken 5 Sample Gameplay

NBA Street: Homecourt

This street basketball based video game simulates basketball in which opponent taunting and official NBA playing style is disregarded. The star of the game is none other than Carmelo Anthony, and within the Game you are able to play against all-time greats like Richard Hamilton, as well as fictional Characters such as Stretch and Bonafide.

NBA Street Homecourt Sample Gameplay

MarioKart: Double Dash

This Mario themed Go-kart racing game much like Super Smash Bros Melee includes characters like Mario, Donkey Kong, Peach and Luigi. The characters race on various tracks, in which they have power-ups they can use against each other.

MarioKart Double Dash Sample Gameplay

Call Of Duty 4: Modern warfare

This first person shooter based video game simulates multiplayer gameplay be it online, or local, and includes various combat options like team death-match, free-for-all, and capture the flag.

Call Of Duty 4:modern warfare Sample Gameplay

Super Smash Bros: Melee

This nintendo based video game simulates 1v1 battles using characters like Mario, Kirby, Zelda, and Donkey Kong.Throught the course of the game, you battle different characters in hopes of unlocking new characters in story mode.
